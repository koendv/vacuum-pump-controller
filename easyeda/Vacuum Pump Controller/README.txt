<p># Controller for pick-and-place machine vacuum pump.</p>
<p>The controller can be used through usb, as part of a pick-and-place machine, or standalone, with a footswitch.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>## Features</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>- up to 4 x BMP280 pressure sensors: 1 for outside pressure, 1 for vacuum pressure, and 2 for two pick-and-place nozzles. The pressure sensors on the nozzles are used to check whether a component has been picked up or has fallen off.&nbsp;</p>
<p>- up to 2 x 12V solenoid valve, one for each pick-and-place nozzle.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>There is an Arduino sketch at <a title="vacuum-pump-controller" href="https://github.com/koendv/vacuum-pump-controller">github</a></p>            
How to use：

At editor, Click the document icon on the topbar, via "Document" > "Open" > "EasyEDA Source", and select json file, then open it at the editor.



如何使用：
